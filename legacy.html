Legacy of the Sands

Overview

A VR historical journey through the founding of the First Saudi State. Built in Unity with narration and immersive 3D environments.

Technologies Used

  • Unity
  • C#
  • VR Interaction

Role in Development

Designed the entire scene flow and historical timeline events with realistic interactions and voice narration.

Challenges

Bringing historical authenticity while using modern VR tools to create an educational yet immersive experience.
