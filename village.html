The Secret of the Village

Overview

An interactive story-driven mystery set in an old Arabian village. Players uncover secrets and solve environmental puzzles tied to the village's forgotten past.

Technologies Used

  • Unity
  • C#
  • 3D Level Design

Role in Development

Designed the environment layout and mystery flow, created clues and interactions that guide players through a gradual narrative revelation.

Challenges

Balancing storytelling and exploration, ensuring the player remains curious and engaged while discovering the hidden history of the village.
