The Morgue

Overview

A chilling VR experience in a morgue where players uncover the story behind abandoned corpses using forensic tools.

Technologies Used

  • Unity
  • Meta SDK
  • VR Physics

Role in Development

Implemented interactive tools, horror pacing, and environmental audio cues to build tension.

Challenges

Creating emotional impact without jump scares, using psychological tension and story pacing.
