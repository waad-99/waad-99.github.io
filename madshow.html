Madshow

Overview

A psychological horror VR experience inside an eerie mental hospital. The player unravels a twisted narrative while solving interactive puzzles.

Technologies Used

  • Unity
  • VR SDK
  • C#
  • Audio Design

Role in Development

Designed immersive horror sequences and handled audio transitions and environmental triggers.

Challenges

Balancing tension with pacing and making interactions feel natural in the VR environment.
