Captain Bonzo

Overview

A mixed reality adventure using Meta Quest hand tracking. Players assist Captain Bonzo in navigating a cartoonish island full of puzzles.

Technologies Used

  • Unity
  • Meta SDK
  • C#
  • XR Interaction Toolkit

Role in Development

Built hand interactions, puzzle logic, and animated events with audio feedback.

Challenges

Optimizing the interaction detection for hand tracking and balancing gameplay difficulty.
